Tattoo Policies & FAQ

Please review these policies before submitting a tattoo request. Booking an appointment indicates that you understand and agree to the policies outlined below.

These policies allow us to respect both our clients and our artists’ time while maintaining a smooth booking process. If you have any questions about these policies, please ask your artist before sending your deposit.

Deposit Policy

A $100 deposit is required to secure all tattoo appointments. Deposits are applied toward the final cost of your tattoo at your last session.

  • Deposits are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  • Your appointment is not secured until the deposit has been received.
  • The deposit will be deducted from the final price of your tattoo.

Your deposit may be forfeited in the following situations:

  • You cancel your appointment.
  • You do not show up for your appointment.
  • You reschedule with less than the notice required by your artist (typically 72 hours – 1 week).
  • You reschedule more than once.
  • You arrive more than 15 minutes late to your appointment.
  • You arrive unfit to be tattooed.
  • You significantly change the tattoo concept originally booked.

If you no-show a consultation appointment, half of your deposit will be forfeited.

Touch-Up Policy

One complimentary touch-up may be provided if necessary for minor healing inconsistencies.

  • Touch-ups must be scheduled within 6 months of the original tattoo appointment.
  • Aftercare instructions must have been followed.
  • Touch-ups are intended for small healed areas only, not redesigns or changes.

Important: Tattoos on the hands, fingers, and feet heal unpredictably due to skin regeneration. These tattoos are not guaranteed and do not include a free touch-up.

  • Touch-ups for these areas or tattoos older than 6 months are subject to a $50 touch-up fee.
  • If you no-show your touch-up appointment, the complimentary touch-up will be forfeited.

Studio Policy

  • Respectful behavior toward all staff and clients is expected at all times.
  • Anyone under the influence of drugs or alcohol will not be tattooed.
  • Children are not permitted inside the studio.
  • Guests may attend appointments if approved by your artist and if they remain respectful and unobtrusive.
  • All staff reserve the right to refuse service if necessary, even after a session has begun.

Consultation Policy

Consultations are offered when needed for larger or more complex tattoo projects.

  • Consultations may be done in person or virtually depending on the project.
  • Please bring reference images, placement ideas, and sizing information when possible.
  • If you no-show your consultation appointment, half of your deposit will be forfeited.

Billing Policy

Tattoo services are billed at the artist’s current hourly rate unless a flat price has been agreed upon.

  • Billing begins once the tattoo session has started.
  • If you arrive late, that time may be included in the billable session time.
  • Pricing varies based on size, placement, detail, and complexity.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • When will I see my tattoo design?
    Most designs are finalized close to the appointment date. This allows the artist to focus on the artwork and ensures we can make any final adjustments together during your session.

  • How do I book an appointment?
    Appointments are requested through the booking form. Once your request is reviewed, you will be contacted with scheduling options.

  • Is a deposit required?
    Yes. A $100 non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment.

  • How much will my tattoo cost?
    Pricing depends on size, placement, detail, and time required. An estimate will be provided after your booking request is reviewed.

  • What should I do before my appointment?
    • Eat a good meal beforehand • Stay hydrated • Get a good night's sleep • Avoid alcohol or drugs before your appointment • Wear comfortable clothing that allows access to the tattoo area

  • What should I bring?
    • Valid government-issued ID • Snacks or drinks for longer sessions • Comfortable clothing appropriate for the tattoo placement

  • What happens if I arrive late?
    If you arrive more than 15 minutes late, your appointment may need to be rescheduled and your deposit may be forfeited.

  • Who should not get tattooed?
    You may be unable to receive a tattoo if you arrive intoxicated, without valid ID, pregnant, or with damaged skin such as sunburn, cuts, or bruising.

  • Do you copy other artists’ tattoos?
    No. The artist creates original designs but can use reference images as inspiration.

  • Do you accept walk-ins?
    Most tattoos are done by appointment so proper time can be dedicated to each piece.

  • How do I care for my tattoo after the session?
    Detailed aftercare instructions will be provided after your appointment to ensure proper healing.
