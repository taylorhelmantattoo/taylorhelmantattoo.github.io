Your Privacy

The information you provide on this form is used only for appointment coordination and record-keeping as required by standard tattoo studio practice.

  • Your responses are stored securely through the form provider and are never sold or shared with third parties.
  • Health disclosures are treated as confidential and are only used to ensure your session is safe.
  • Photo consent is opt-in. Declining does not affect your appointment in any way.
